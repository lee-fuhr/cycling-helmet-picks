A cycling helmet is the single most important piece of safety gear you own. Whether you’re a weekend cruiser, a daily commuter, or a weekend warrior, a well-fitting helmet with modern protection technology like MIPS (Multi-directional Impact Protection System) can significantly reduce the risk of serious head injury. Choosing the right helmet comes down to three things: fit, ventilation, and budget. A helmet should sit level on your head with the straps snug under your chin. Look for ample vents and internal channeling to keep you cool on warm rides. MIPS adds rotational protection and is now widely available even at mid-range prices.
