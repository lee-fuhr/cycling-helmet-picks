How we choose our picks

We don’t run a lab or crash-test helmets ourselves. Instead, we sift through thousands of long-term owner reviews, study published expert tests from reputable sources like Virginia Tech’s helmet ratings, and track real-world failure reports, stripped retention knobs, broken straps, foams that degrade too quickly. Over time, patterns emerge: which models consistently protect well, which fit true to size, and which fall apart after a season of sun exposure. We also weigh anecdotal feedback from mechanics, bike shop staff, and club riders who see these helmets day in and day out. A helmet that scores high in the lab but feels flimsy in daily use doesn’t earn a spot here. Our final picks are those that marry proven impact protection with honest durability and a fit that actually works for a wide range of head shapes, no invented specs, no hype, just the stuff that holds up.
